📝 OBT Quality Evaluation Criteria:
  • Content Fidelity: Are all events from the map present? Any additions?
  • Participant Clarity: Are roles and relations clear to a listener?
  • Emotional Content: Are the emotions from the map conveyed?
  • Oral Markers: Are attentional, structural, and turn-taking markers appropriate?
  • Subtraction Test: Do markers add info, or only manage the channel?
  • Natural Flow: Does it sound like oral storytelling in the target language?
  • Map Gaps: What's missing from the map that the composer needed?
